Course Details

SIS / English Language Training

English Language Training

About The Course

This course includes all four language skills Speaking, Listening; Reading & Writing so that students will be able to communicate fluently in the formal and informal situations, develop their reading skills as well as improve writing skills for oral and written communication. It also helps them to learn reading and writing techniques along with development of presentation abilities which are crucial to communication. It also includes individual and group presentation on a given/ selected topic. As a result, students will be able to make themselves more confident in freelancing career.

Course Module

Formal Meeting
Vocabulary Practice
Dialogue Practice
Listening Practice
Preposition
Formal Presentation
Phonetics
Tense Practice
Demo Conversation with client
Business Etiquette

Duration

Total Class - 18

(2 hours a Day, 2 Days in a Week)

Learning Outcomes

Listen to English command and respond accordingly
Present their project in front of a group of people
Conducting simple phone conversations
Describe the basic words/ Phrases to describe “Myself”
Socialize and get introduced in the Business Context
Describe previous experience or moment sharing activities
Improve vocabulary skills
Correct English Pronunciation
